## Hello, World! 👋

Welcome to my blog! This is where I share my thoughts, learnings, and technical write-ups on topics I'm passionate about. Whether it's cybersecurity, Linux tools, web development, or just interesting things I've discovered — you'll find it here.

Here's a quick overview of the kind of content you'll find on this blog:

- **Tool Explanations** — Deep dives into developer and security tools
- **Technical Tutorials** — Step-by-step guides for common tasks
- **Personal Insights** — Lessons learned from real-world experience

## Why Static?

I chose a static site generator because:

1. **Performance** — Static sites are blazing fast
2. **Security** — No server-side code means a smaller attack surface
3. **Simplicity** — Markdown files are easy to write and maintain
4. **Cost** — Free hosting on platforms like Netlify

> "The best blog is the one you actually write on." — Someone wise, probably.
